================================================================================
  SERVICES USED
================================================================================

  LLM (decompose, extract, gap-check, synthesize):
      Qwen3-235B @ http://69.48.159.8:30005/v1

  Embedding (query vectors):
      BAAI/bge-m3 @ http://69.48.159.8:30007/v1 (1024-dim)

  Vector Store (hybrid BM25 + kNN):
      OpenSearch 3.4 @ localhost:9200
      - pdf_doc_index   (coarse doc-level filter)
      - pdf_chunk_index (fine chunk-level retrieval)

  Orchestration:
      LangGraph (Command/Send fan-out, Annotated fan-in)

================================================================================
  KEY PARAMETERS
================================================================================

  Chunking:     500 tokens, 50-token overlap, ~4 chars/token
  Doc filter:   top 20 candidates
  Chunk search: top 50 -> re-rank to 15
  BM25 weight:  0.3
  kNN weight:   0.7
  Context:      expand +/- 2 pages around matched chunks
  Gap-fill:     max 2 loop iterations
  Max context:  80,000 tokens budget
